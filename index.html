What is New Sole?


New Sole is a new place to buy and sell sneakers. But because resell culture can get expensive, you have the option to pay differently. Of course, you can pay with money, but here at New Sole, we also give you the opportunity of trading shoes. You can contact and talk to a seller or buyer and reach an agreement for trade.

The idea behind New Sole.


We have these kinds of payment options because we want to give more people the chance to own/buy more exclusive sneakers. Often, when exclusive sneakers get released, they release in limited pairs. So the only way to get an opportunity to buy them is by entering a raffle. But many people use bots to increase their chances of winning, so we want to try and even the playing grounds.
